INFLATION CALCULATOR

The Inflation Calculator was created by MÃ¡rio Braga as the final project for the "Advanced Data Journalism and Coding" module taught by Basile Simon at City, University of London. It was part of the Erasmus Mundus Master Programme in Journalism, Media and Globalisation.

The main goal of this project is to provide the reader with a basic understanding of price dynamics and how inflation rates are calculated. Moreover, the possibility to find their specific inflation rate via the interactive tool aims to raise awarness for the nuances often eclipsed by economic indicators.

All the data used to plot the charts presented in the homepage and in the calculator itself are from the Brazilian national statistics agency, IBGE. You can access that information here, or click here to download the datasets and codes used to build this page and the interactive calculator.
