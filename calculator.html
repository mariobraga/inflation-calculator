INFLATION CALCULATOR

Now that you know that each item from the basket of goods selected by IBGE has a different impact on the overall IPCA rate, it is time to find out what was the inflation of the products you buy.

The logic is simple: if a customer does not buy one of the products that got more expensive, the overall inflation of their basket of goods will be different than the average rate - in this case lower. However, if they skip the ones that got cheaper, their average price increase will be higher than the official rate.

The Inflation Calculator allows you to select the products you buy by clicking on the items below so you discover what was your inflation rate in 2018. After clicking on all the items you normally consume, select "Calculate My Inflation" to see the result.
